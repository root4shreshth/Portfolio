About Me

I am a passionate and creative Frontend Developer with a strong foundation in building user-friendly and visually engaging web interfaces. Currently pursuing a B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering, I bring hands-on experience with core web technologies including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I’m driven by the challenge of transforming complex problems into elegant, intuitive digital experiences.

With a deep interest in UI/UX, I combine technical skill with a design-first mindset, always aiming to deliver seamless and effective user experiences.
